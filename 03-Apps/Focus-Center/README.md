# Focus Center

## Overview

Focus Center is the home page of Moer OS.

It helps Jim immediately understand what deserves his attention today without opening multiple Apps.

Instead of managing tasks, Focus Center helps Jim make better daily decisions.

The goal is simple:

**Open one page. Know exactly what to do next.**

---

## Purpose

Reduce decision fatigue.

Save time.

Keep business and family organized.

Help Jim focus on what matters most today.

---

## Target User

Primary User:

* Jim

Future Users:

* Moer Sourcing Team

---

## Core Questions

Every time Jim opens Focus Center, it should answer these questions within 30 seconds:

1. What should I do today?
2. Who needs me today?
3. Is anything urgent?
4. Do I need to film or post today?
5. What's for dinner?
6. Is there anything important for my son today?

---

## Success

If Jim can open Focus Center every morning and immediately know what deserves his attention, the app is successful.
