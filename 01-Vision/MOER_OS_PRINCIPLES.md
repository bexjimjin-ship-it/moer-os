# Moer OS Principles - 1

## Mission

Build one intelligent system that helps run both business and family, so time can be spent creating instead of remembering.

---

## Principle 1

The system remembers.
Jim creates.

---

## Principle 2

Every App solves one problem extremely well.

---

## Principle 3

If it doesn't save time, we don't build it.

---

## Principle 4

Everything important should be reachable within one or two clicks.

---

## Principle 5

Simple beats complicated.

---

## Principle 6

Business and Family are separate spaces, but live inside one trusted system.

---

## Principle 7

The system should reduce stress, not create more work.

---

## Principle 8

Every new App must answer one clear question.

Example:

Tasks:
What should I do today?

CRM:
Who should I follow up with today?

Meal Planner:
What are we eating today?

Supplier:
Which supplier needs my attention?

Quotation:
Which quotation is waiting?

---

## Principle 9

Automation is preferred over repetition.

---

## Principle 10

Build for everyday use, not for demonstration.
